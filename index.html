6-Day Workout Plan (Sirf Dumbbells ke Saath)

Day 1: Chest, Shoulders, and Punch Power

  • 1. Dumbbell Floor Press:
  • 3 sets x 12 reps
  • 2. Dumbbell Front Raise:
  • 3 sets x 10 reps
  • 3. Dumbbell Push-Ups (Dumbbells as handles):
  • 3 sets x Max reps
  • 4. Dumbbell Shadow Boxing (2-3 kg dumbbells):
  • 3 rounds x 1 min
  • 5. Dumbbell Lateral Raise:
  • 3 sets x 12 reps

Day 2: Martial Arts Drills

  • 1. Shadow Boxing (No Dumbbells):
  • 3 rounds x 2 min
  • 2. Front Kicks:
  • 3 sets x 15 per leg
  • 3. Side Kicks:
  • 3 sets x 12 per leg
  • 4. Dumbbell Punch Combos (Light Dumbbells):
  • 3 rounds x 1 min
  • 5. Core: Plank Hold with Dumbbell Row:
  • 3 sets x 30-40 sec

Day 3: Back, Biceps, and Grappling Strength

  • 1. Dumbbell Deadlift:
  • 3 sets x 12 reps
  • 2. Dumbbell Bent-Over Row:
  • 3 sets x 12 reps
  • 3. Dumbbell Bicep Curls:
  • 3 sets x 12 reps
  • 4. Dumbbell Hammer Curls:
  • 3 sets x 10 reps
  • 5. Dumbbell Shrugs:
  • 3 sets x 15 reps

Day 4: Legs and Kick Power

  • 1. Goblet Squats (Hold Dumbbell):
  • 3 sets x 15 reps
  • 2. Dumbbell Romanian Deadlifts:
  • 3 sets x 12 reps
  • 3. Dumbbell Lunges:
  • 3 sets x 12 reps per leg
  • 4. Roundhouse Kicks:
  • 3 sets x 10 per leg
  • 5. Jump Squats (With Dumbbells):
  • 3 sets x 12 reps

Day 5: Martial Arts Combinations

  • 1. Shadow Boxing Combos (Jab-Cross-Hook):
  • 3 rounds x 2 min
  • 2. Side Kick + Spinning Back Kick:
  • 3 sets x 10 combos
  • 3. Duck and Counter Punch (With Dumbbells):
  • 3 rounds x 1 min
  • 4. Punch-Kick Combos (Front Kick + Jab-Cross):
  • 3 rounds x 1 min

Martial Arts Tips (Sirf Home Training ke Liye)

  • 1. Punching Form:
  • Mirror ke saamne shadow boxing karo aur speed improve karne ki koshish karo.
  • 2. Kicking Form:
  • Balance aur flexibility ke liye apne kicks ko slow motion me practice karo.
  • 3. Defense:
  • Ducking aur blocking drills karo (duck aur move ke saath counter punch).
  • 4. Reflex Training:
  • Ek tennis ball ko diwaar par maaro aur catch karte raho.

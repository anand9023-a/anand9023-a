4 sets x 10-12 reps

3 sets x 10 reps

3 sets x 12 reps

4 sets x 10 reps

3 sets x 12 reps

3 sets x 12 reps

4 sets x 12 reps

3 sets x 10 reps

4 sets x 10 reps

3 sets x 12 reps

3 sets x 12 reps

4 sets x 12 reps

3 sets x 12 reps

3 sets x 15 reps

4 sets x 10 reps per leg

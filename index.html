Workout Plan

Day 1: Upper Body (Chest, Shoulders, Arms)

  • Push-ups - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Lateral Raise - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Bicep Curl + Triceps Kickback Superset - 3 sets of 12-15 reps each
  • Pull-Up - 3 sets of max reps
  • Forearm Workout (As in video)

Day 2: Lower Body (Legs + Core)

  • Squats - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Lunges - 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
  • Goblet Squat - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Single Leg Calf Raise - 3 sets of 15-20 reps per leg
  • Plank - 3 sets of 30-45 seconds hold
  • Abs Workout

Day 3: Back Focused Workout

  • Dumbbell Deadlift - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Bent Over Row - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Pull-up - 3 sets of max reps
  • Push-up + Dumbbell Row Combo - 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Plank to Push-Up - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Forearm Workout (As in video)

Day 4: Shoulders and Arms

  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Lateral Raise - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Bicep Curl + Triceps Kickback Superset - 3 sets of 12-15 reps each
  • Hammer Curl - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Farmer’s Walk - 1-minute walk (3 times)
  • Abs Workout

Day 5: Lower Body and Core

  • Squats - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Romanian Deadlift - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Lunges - 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
  • Goblet Squat - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Single Leg Calf Raise - 3 sets of 15-20 reps per leg
  • Plank - 3 sets of 30-45 seconds hold
  • Forearm Workout (As in video)

Day 6: Full Body Workout

  • Dumbbell Deadlift - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Bent Over Row - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Push-up + Dumbbell Row Combo - 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Hammer Curl - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Farmer’s Walk - 1-minute walk (3 times)
  • Plank to Push-Up - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Abs Workout

Day 7: Rest Day (Sunday)

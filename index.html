Eye Exercises Routine

Daily Eye Exercises

  • Pencil Push-Up Exercise:
  • 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Focus Change Exercise:
  • 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Eye Rolling Exercise:
  • 10 circles clockwise, 10 circles anti-clockwise (3 sets)
  • Near and Far Focus Exercise:
  • 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Palming Exercise:
  • 1-2 minutes (3 times)
  • Blinking Exercise:
  • 1 minute (3 sets)

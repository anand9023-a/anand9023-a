Home Workout Plan

Target: Build a Body Like a Pro

Day 1: Chest & Triceps

  • Push-Ups – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Incline Push-Ups – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Diamond Push-Ups – 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Floor Press – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Overhead Dumbbell Tricep Extension – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 2: Back & Biceps

  • Dumbbell Bent-Over Rows – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Renegade Rows – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Pull-Ups – 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Dumbbell Bicep Curls – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Hammer Curls – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 3: Legs & Abs

  • Bodyweight Squats – 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Dumbbell Goblet Squats – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Romanian Deadlifts – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Standing Calf Raises – 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Plank Hold – 3 sets of 30-60 seconds

Day 4: Shoulders & Abs

  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Lateral Raises – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Front Raises – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Shrugs – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Russian Twists – 3 sets of 20 twists

Day 5: Chest, Back & Arms

  • Wide Grip Push-Ups – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Bent-Over Rows – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Bicep Curls – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Tricep Dips – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Plank with Shoulder Taps – 3 sets of 20 taps

Day 6: Full Body & Abs

  • Dumbbell Deadlift – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Clean & Press – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Farmer’s Walk – 3 sets of 20-30 seconds
  • Mountain Climbers – 3 sets of 20-30 seconds
  • Leg Raises – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

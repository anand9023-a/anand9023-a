🏋️ Full Home Workout Plan (Dumbbells Only)

Baki the Grappler Style – 6 Days a Week

📅 Weekly Workout Split

Day 1 – Chest & Triceps | Day 2 – Back, Biceps & Forearms | Day 3 – Shoulders & Neck

Day 4 – Legs & Core | Day 5 – Arms & Forearms | Day 6 – Full Body + Neck & Jawline

Day 7 – Rest

🔴 Day 1: Chest & Triceps

  • ✅ Push-ups – 4 sets × 15 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Floor Press – 4 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Chest Flys – 4 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Triceps Extensions – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Diamond Push-ups – 3 sets × 12 reps

🔵 Day 2: Back, Biceps & Forearms

  • ✅ Dumbbell Bent-over Rows – 4 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Reverse Flys – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Hammer Curls – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Wrist Curls (Palm Up) – 3 sets × 15 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Reverse Wrist Curls (Palm Down) – 3 sets × 15 reps
  • ✅ Farmer’s Walk – 3 sets × 30 sec

🟡 Day 3: Shoulders & Neck

  • ✅ Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 4 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Lateral Raises – 4 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Front Raises – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Shrugs – 4 sets × 15 reps
  • ✅ Neck Flexion – 3 sets × 15 reps
  • ✅ Neck Extension – 3 sets × 15 reps
  • ✅ Neck Side Bends – 3 sets × 15 reps

🟢 Day 4: Legs & Core

  • ✅ Dumbbell Squats – 4 sets × 15 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Lunges – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Romanian Deadlifts – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Seated Calf Raises – 3 sets × 15 reps
  • ✅ Hanging Leg Raises – 3 sets × 15 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Russian Twists – 3 sets × 20 reps

🟠 Day 5: Arms & Forearms

  • ✅ Dumbbell Bicep Curls – 4 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Hammer Curls – 4 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Triceps Kickbacks – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Wrist Curls – 3 sets × 15 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Reverse Wrist Curls – 3 sets × 15 reps
  • ✅ Plate Pinch Hold – 3 sets × 30 sec

🟣 Day 6: Full Body + Neck & Jawline

  • ✅ Dumbbell Snatch – 4 sets × 10 reps
  • ✅ Dumbbell Deadlifts – 4 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Push-ups (Explosive) – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • ✅ Farmer’s Walk – 3 sets × 30 sec
  • ✅ Neck Flexion – 3 sets × 15 reps
  • ✅ Neck Side Bends – 3 sets × 15 reps
  • ✅ **Jawline Work (Mewing & Chewing) – Daily

🛑 Extra Tips for Baki-Style Body

🔹 Forearms – Add wrist curls, farmer’s walk & pinch holds for a thicker look

🔹 Neck – Train front, back & side neck muscles to build thickness

🔹 Jawline – Practice mewing & chewing harder foods for a sharper face

🔥 Follow this routine consistently & within 3-4 months, you’ll see big changes! 💪🔥

6-Day Dumbbell Workout Plan

Day 1: Upper Body Strength & Hypertrophy
1. Dumbbell Floor Press:
4 sets x 8-12 reps
2. Dumbbell Shoulder Press:
3 sets x 10-12 reps
3. Dumbbell Bent-over Rows:
3 sets x 12-15 reps
4. Dumbbell Lateral Raises:
3 sets x 15 reps
5. Push-ups:
3 sets x 15-20 reps
Day 2: Lower Body Strength & Endurance
1. Goblet Squats:
4 sets x 10-12 reps
2. Dumbbell Romanian Deadlifts:
3 sets x 10-12 reps
3. Dumbbell Calf Raises:
3 sets x 20 reps
4. Jump Squats (No Weight):
3 sets x 15 reps
5. Plank Hold:
3 sets x 45-60 sec
Day 3: Full Body Functional Strength
1. Dumbbell Thrusters (Squat + Shoulder Press):
3 sets x 12 reps
2. Dumbbell Renegade Rows:
3 sets x 10 reps per arm
3. Dumbbell Bulgarian Split Squats:
3 sets x 10 reps per leg
4. Dumbbell Swings:
3 sets x 12 reps
5. Burpees:
3 sets x 12 reps
Day 4: Upper Body Strength & Hypertrophy

(Same as Day 1 - Try increasing reps or weights)

Day 5: Lower Body Strength & Endurance

(Same as Day 2 - Focus on improving form)

Day 6: Active Recovery & Endurance
1. Jump Rope / Shadow Boxing:
10-15 mins
2. Core Workout:
- Crunches: 3 sets x 15 reps
- Leg Raises: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
- Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets x 15 reps
- Plank Hold: 3 sets x 30-45 sec
3. Stretching & Mobility:
- Shoulder Stretch
- Chest Opener
- Cat-Cow Stretch
- Hamstring Stretch
- Hip Flexor Stretch

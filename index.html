Weekly Workout Plan

Day 1: Upper Body (Chest, Shoulders, Arms)

1. Push-ups - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
2. Dumbbell Shoulder Press - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
3. Lateral Raise - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
4. Bicep Curl + Triceps Kickback Superset - 3 sets of 12-15 reps each
5. Pull-Up - 3 sets of max reps
6. Forearm workout (As shown in video)

Day 2: Lower Body (Legs + Core)

1. Squats - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
2. Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
3. Dumbbell Lunges - 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
4. Goblet Squat - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
5. Single Leg Calf Raise - 3 sets of 15-20 reps per leg
6. Plank - 3 sets of 30-45 seconds hold
7. Abs workout - 3 sets of 12-15 reps each

Day 3: Back Focused Workout

1. Dumbbell Deadlift - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
2. Dumbbell Bent Over Row - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
3. Pull-up - 3 sets of max reps
4. Push-up + Dumbbell Row Combo - 3 sets of 12 reps
5. Plank to Push-Up - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
6. Forearm workout (As shown in video)

Day 4: Shoulders and Arms

1. Dumbbell Shoulder Press - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
2. Lateral Raise - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
3. Bicep Curl + Triceps Kickback Superset - 3 sets of 12-15 reps each
4. Hammer Curl - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
5. Dumbbell Farmer’s Walk - 1-minute walk (3 times)
6. Abs workout - 3 sets of 12-15 reps each

Day 5: Lower Body and Core

1. Squats - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
2. Romanian Deadlift - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
3. Dumbbell Lunges - 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
4. Goblet Squat - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
5. Single Leg Calf Raise - 3 sets of 15-20 reps per leg
6. Plank - 3 sets of 30-45 seconds hold
7. Forearm workout (As shown in video)

Day 6: Full Body Workout

1. Dumbbell Deadlift - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
2. Dumbbell Bent Over Row - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
3. Push-up + Dumbbell Row Combo - 3 sets of 12 reps
4. Hammer Curl - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
5. Dumbbell Farmer’s Walk - 1-minute walk (3 times)
6. Plank to Push-Up - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
7. Abs workout - 3 sets of 12-15 reps each

Day 7: Rest Day (Sunday)

Take a rest day to allow your body to recover.

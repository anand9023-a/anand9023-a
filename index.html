Workout & Martial Arts Plan

Goal: Build a strong body and learn martial arts.

Equipment: Dumbbells

Day 1: Chest, Shoulders, and Punch Power

  • Dumbbell Floor Press - 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Front Raise - 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Push-Ups (Dumbbells as handles) - 3 sets x Max reps
  • Dumbbell Shadow Boxing (2-3 kg dumbbells) - 3 rounds x 1 min
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raise - 3 sets x 12 reps

Day 2: Martial Arts Drills

  • Shadow Boxing (No Dumbbells) - 3 rounds x 2 min
  • Front Kicks - 3 sets x 15 per leg
  • Side Kicks - 3 sets x 12 per leg
  • Dumbbell Punch Combos (Light Dumbbells) - 3 rounds x 1 min
  • Core: Plank Hold with Dumbbell Row - 3 sets x 30-40 sec

Day 3: Back, Biceps, and Grappling Strength

  • Dumbbell Deadlift - 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Bent-Over Row - 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Bicep Curls - 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Hammer Curls - 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Shrugs - 3 sets x 15 reps

Day 4: Legs and Kick Power

  • Goblet Squats (Hold Dumbbell) - 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Dumbbell Romanian Deadlifts - 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Lunges - 3 sets x 12 reps per leg
  • Roundhouse Kicks - 3 sets x 10 per leg
  • Jump Squats (With Dumbbells) - 3 sets x 12 reps

Day 5: Martial Arts Combinations

  • Shadow Boxing Combos (Jab-Cross-Hook) - 3 rounds x 2 min
  • Side Kick + Spinning Back Kick - 3 sets x 10 combos
  • Duck and Counter Punch (With Dumbbells) - 3 rounds x 1 min
  • Punch-Kick Combos (Front Kick + Jab-Cross) - 3 rounds x 1 min
  • Cooldown: Stretching (Hamstrings, Quads, Shoulders)

Day 6: Core Strength and Speed

  • Dumbbell Russian Twists - 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Leg Raises (With Dumbbell Between Feet) - 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Mountain Climbers - 3 sets x 30 sec

  • Agility Drills (Shadow Boxing with Fast Movement) - 3 rounds x 1 min

Day 7: Rest

Light jogging, stretching, or yoga for recovery.

Created for a balanced body and martial arts skills.

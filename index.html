6-Day Full Body Workout Plan with Forearm Focus

Day 1: Chest & Triceps

  • Wide Hand Push-Up – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Incline Push-Up – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Decline Push-Up – 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Diamond Push-Up – 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Standing One Arm Extension – 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 2: Back, Biceps & Forearms

  • Dumbbell Deadlift – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Bent Over Row – 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Pullover on Floor – 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Pull-Ups (Assisted, if needed) – 3 sets of max reps
  • Dumbbell Alternate Biceps Curl – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Reverse Curls (Forearm Focus) – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Wrist Curls (Forearm Focus) – 3 sets of 15-20 reps

Day 3: Legs & Abs

  • Bodyweight Squat – 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Dumbbell Goblet Squat – 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Lunge – 3 sets of 12 reps (each leg)
  • Romanian Deadlift – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Standing Single Leg Calf Raise – 3 sets of 20 reps (each leg)
  • Plank Hold – 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
  • Leg Raises – 3 sets of 15 reps

Day 4: Shoulders & Forearms

  • Dumbbell One Arm Shoulder Press – 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raise – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Rear Lateral Raise – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Front Raise – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Farmer’s Walk (Forearm Focus) – 3 sets of 30-40 seconds
  • Reverse Dumbbell Wrist Curls (Forearm Focus) – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 5: Chest, Back & Arms

  • Wide Hand Push-Up – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Incline Push-Up – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Pull-Ups (Assisted) – 3 sets of max reps
  • Dumbbell Bent Over Row – 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Alternate Biceps Curl – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Reverse Curls (Forearm Focus) – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 6: Full Body, Abs & Forearms

  • Dumbbell Deadlift – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-Up – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Pullover on Floor – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Bodyweight Squat – 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Plank Hold – 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
  • Dumbbell Hammer Curls (Forearm Focus) – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dead Hangs (Optional, Forearm Focus) – 2 sets of 30-40 seconds

Day 7: Rest/Active Recovery

  • Light stretching or yoga
  • Walk for 15-20 minutes

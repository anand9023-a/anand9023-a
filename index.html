6-Day Workout Plan

Day 1: Chest & Back

  • Diamond Push-ups - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Incline Push-ups - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Decline Push-ups - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Wide Push-ups - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Bent Over Row - 3 sets of 10-15 reps

Day 2: Legs & Shoulders

  • Squats - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Lunges (each leg) - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Lateral Raise - 3 sets of 10-15 reps

Day 3: Arms & Forearms

  • Dumbbell Bicep Curl - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Tricep Kickback - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Hammer Curl - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Wrist Curls - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Reverse Dumbbell Wrist Curls - 3 sets of 15-20 reps

Day 4: Chest & Back (Light)

  • Incline Push-ups - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Chest Fly - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Deadlift - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Shrug - 3 sets of 10-15 reps

Day 5: Legs, Calves & Shoulders (Light)

  • Goblet Squat - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Standing Calf Raises - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Dumbbell Front Raise - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Rear Lateral Raise - 3 sets of 10-15 reps

Day 6: Core & Obliques

  • Plank - 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
  • Russian Twists (each side) - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Side Plank (each side) - 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Bicycle Crunches - 3 sets of 15-20 reps

Day 7: Rest Day

No exercise. Focus on recovery.

Designed for beginners to intermediate level. Stay consistent and enjoy your workouts!

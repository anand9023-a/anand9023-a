Workout Routine

Day 1: Chest, Back & Traps (Upper Body Strength)
Dumbbell Floor Press 4 sets x 10-12 reps
Incline Push-ups (Upper Chest) 3 sets x 15 reps
Dumbbell Chest Flys 3 sets x 12 reps
Pull-ups (or Negative Pull-ups) 4 sets x 6-10 reps
Dumbbell Bent-over Rows 4 sets x 12 reps
Dumbbell Pullover (Lats & Chest) 3 sets x 12 reps
Dumbbell Shrugs (Traps) 3 sets x 15 reps
Day 2: Legs & Shoulders (Lower Body Power + Shoulder Size)
Goblet Squat (Quads & Glutes) 4 sets x 12 reps
Romanian Deadlifts (Hamstrings & Glutes) 3 sets x 12 reps
Bulgarian Split Squats (Balance & Legs) 3 sets x 10 reps (each leg)
Standing Calf Raises 3 sets x 20 reps
Dumbbell Shoulder Press 4 sets x 10-12 reps
Dumbbell Lateral Raises (Width) 3 sets x 12 reps
Face Pulls (Posterior Delts & Posture) 3 sets x 15 reps
Day 3: Arms, Forearms & Grip (Bigger & Stronger Arms)
Dumbbell Bicep Curls 3 sets x 12 reps
Hammer Curls (Forearm & Biceps) 3 sets x 12 reps
Dumbbell Concentration Curls 3 sets x 12 reps
Dumbbell Triceps Kickbacks 3 sets x 12 reps
Dumbbell Overhead Triceps Extension 3 sets x 12 reps
Dumbbell Wrist Curls (Palm Up) 3 sets x 15 reps
Dumbbell Reverse Wrist Curls (Palm Down) 3 sets x 15 reps
Farmer’s Walk (Grip Strength) 3 sets x 30 sec
Day 4: Chest, Back & Neck (Upper Body Symmetry)
Diamond Push-ups (Triceps & Inner Chest) 3 sets x 12 reps
Wide Push-ups (Outer Chest) 3 sets x 12 reps
Dumbbell Floor Press 3 sets x 12 reps
Dumbbell Bent-over Rows 3 sets x 12 reps
Dumbbell Deadlifts (Full Back & Legs) 3 sets x 10 reps
Dumbbell Shrugs 3 sets x 15 reps
Dumbbell Neck Flexion (Front Neck) 3 sets x 15 reps
Dumbbell Neck Extension (Back Neck) 3 sets x 15 reps
Neck Side Bends (Jawline & Neck Thickness) 3 sets x 15 reps
Day 5: Legs, Calves & Shoulders (Leg Aesthetics & Shoulder Strength)
Goblet Squat 4 sets x 12 reps
Dumbbell Lunges 3 sets x 10 reps (each leg)
Standing Calf Raises 3 sets x 20 reps
Seated Calf Raises (Use Dumbbells on Knees) 3 sets x 20 reps
Dumbbell Front Raises (Front Delts) 3 sets x 12 reps
Dumbbell Rear Lateral Raises (Rear Delts) 3 sets x 12 reps
Plate Pinch Hold (Grip Strength) 3 sets x 30 sec
Day 6: Core, Abs & Jawline (Aesthetic Core & Defined Face)
Hanging Leg Raises (Use Table/Bed Support) 3 sets x 12 reps
Russian Twists (With Dumbbell) 3 sets x 15 reps
Side Plank (Obliques & Core Stability) 3 sets x 30 sec
Bicycle Crunches 3 sets x 15 reps
Dumbbell Side Bends (Obliques & Waistline) 3 sets x 12 reps
Mewing & Hard Chewing Practice Daily

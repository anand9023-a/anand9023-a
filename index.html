4-5 Month Transformation Plan

1. Workout Plan

  • Subah Workout (40-45 min):
    • Warm-up: Silent jumping jacks (5 min).
    • Strength Training: Target chest, back, abs, arms, legs.
    • End with Push-ups, Squats, and Sit-ups (2 sets each).
  • Diet:
    • Breakfast: Oats, peanut butter, banana, milk/boiled eggs.
    • Lunch: Roti, sabji, dal, chawal, chaas.
    • Snacks: Dry fruits + fruit (banana/apple).
    • Dinner: 2-3 roti + sabji + salad.
    • Pre-bed: Milk with turmeric or protein shake.

2. Skin Care Routine

  • Morning: Face wash, moisturizer, sunscreen.
  • Night: Face wash, aloe vera gel, or vitamin C serum.
  • Weekly: Turmeric-honey mask (2x/week) + scrubbing.
  • Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily.

3. Hair Care

  • Shampoo 2-3 times a week (mild shampoo).
  • Oil massage (coconut/onion oil) 2 times a week.
  • Apply egg + curd mask (2x/month).

4. Grooming

  • Use castor oil for beard growth (if needed).
  • Get a stylish haircut.
  • Maintain hygiene: Trim nails, use deodorant.

5. Personal Development

  • Read daily for 10-15 minutes (self-help or articles).
  • Improve communication skills (practice new words).
  • Build confidence with mirror practice.

Stay Consistent & Confident!

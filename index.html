6-Day Workout Plan

Workout Notes
  • Sets/Reps: 4 sets, 12–20 reps (agar easy lage to slow tempo + pause).
  • Rest: 45–60 sec.
  • Total Time: ~1 hour.
  • Duration: 6 din har week repeat karne layak, 100% effective.
